CAN GLOBAL ENERGY SYSTEMS SUSTAIN THE RAPID GROWTH OF AI?


======================================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

KEY FINDINGS

1. AI demand remains a relatively small share of total global electricity demand.

2. Regional grid readiness is more important than global electricity supply.

3. Renewable-rich countries are best positioned for AI expansion.

4. Carbon-intensive systems face higher risk.

5. Grid infrastructure investment will become increasingly important.
